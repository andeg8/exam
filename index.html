What Are Solar Flares?

Definition: A brief eruption of intense high-energy radiation from the sun's surface,
associated with sunspots and causing electromagnetic disturbances on the earth,
as with radio frequency communications and power line transmissions.


How are Solar Flares formed?

A solar flare occurs when magnetic energy that has built up in the solar atmosphere is suddenly released.


What is a CME?

A CME or Coronal Mass Ejection follows a solar flare and is when a piece of the sun breaks off and explodes.

What could a CME do?

The effects of a massive CME could destroy the Earth's atmosphere. It was recently discovered that if a planet loses it's magnetic field it's atmosphere will dissipate. This is what is believed by some to have happened to mars after a solar flare and CME hit it.


Solar Storm of 2012

The solar storm of 2012 was the biggest recorded CME ever. It was extremely close to hitting earth, although it didn’t.
A study says “...the recovery time from such a disaster would have been about four to ten years.”
